Accommodation


A limited number of rooms in low-cost (“subsidised”) accommodation will be made available. All rooms will be basic, en-suite rooms in Sir Charles Groves Hall, a student accommodation building located less than 10 minutes walk from the meeting venue. If interested in this option, you must indicate this when you register (before Friday 21st February, link above), or contact the LOC before Friday 21st February. Further details about the accommodation will be provided here shortly. Some further hotels around Manchester city centre suggested by the LOC include:

  • Pendulum Hotel
  • Premier Inn - Manchester Central , Manchester City Centre (Portland Street), Manchester City Centre (Princess Street)
  • Motel One, Piccadilly
  • Ibis - Manchester Centre, Manchester Styles.
We are looking into having a number of rooms set aside at the Ibis Manchester Styles for attendees of the Meeting. We will post any updates here in due course. A comprehensive list of further accommodation options can be found on Visit Manchester, or on Booking.com.

PLEASE NOTE!
Prices and availability for hotels on Sunday 20th July may be in high demand due to the Oasis reunion tour in Manchester. Please be aware that the cost of a hotel room may be inflated on the Sunday night, but should be more affordable Monday-Friday during the week. We encourage in-person attendees to book their accommodation as soon as possible.

Venue


The Meeting will take place in the Schuster Building at the University of Manchester, Manchester M13 9PL. We will use the Rutherford Lecture Theatre, Jocelyn Bell Burnell Lecture Theatre, and Schuster Foyer. There will also be smaller meeting rooms available for personal use and free discussion.

Travel


Manchester is linked by rail to a range of major cities across the UK, and is served by Manchester Airport. For general travel guidance to the city, including from airports other than Manchester, please follow this link to advice provided by the University of Manchester.

The main University campus is easily accessible by foot or bus from Manchester city centre.
